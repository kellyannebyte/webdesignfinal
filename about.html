What is this?

As stated on our home page, this website is a final project put together to replicate what a normal website of an Equestrian Center would look like!

Viridity Equestrian Center was initially created by Kelly Anne Williams, a Junior at New York University, as a project to showcase Web Design skills in hopes to score a commission for equestrian businesses in the real world. In addition, this example of a website can be applied to video game based communities for the near future.

Is this real?

Obviously not. It's kind of a "make believe" scenario of an Equestrian Center and all aspects of it were created by imagination. As someone who plays video games, I enjoy coming up with different ideas and concepts of alternate realities.

Most images used are from the video game, Star Stable Online.

What the heck is Star Stable?

Star Stable Online is a video game oriented towards a younger audience interested in horses. In its multiplayer open world, it consists of races, quests, custom characterization, and interaction with other players.

I bought a lifetime membership in 2013... I think I was 11 years old. Until a couple months ago, I didn't realize the game was still running and my lifetime membership still worked! It was like my Poptropica or Club Penguin growing up.
